Welcome to Task Flow

Task Flow is your personal internship assistant designed to help interns stay organized, consistent, and productive. Record your daily and weekly tasks, track your progress over time, and receive feedback from your supervisor all in one simple, user-friendly dashboard.

Whether you’re managing your first internship or leading a team project, Task Flow helps you focus on what matters most learning, improving, and achieving your professional goals with clarity and confidence.

Daily & Weekly Task Management

Easily add, update, and track your tasks every day to stay on top of your goals.

Progress Tracking

Visualize your growth with progress charts and performance insights.

Supervisor Review

Supervisors can review and approve your progress, making feedback effortless.

Smart Reminders

Get timely reminders to complete tasks before deadlines.
